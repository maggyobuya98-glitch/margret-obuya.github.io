Research agenda
1. AI governance in African supply chains
How is AI deployment in agricultural and fisheries supply chains in sub-Saharan Africa currently governed? What harms have already emerged, and what frameworks, adapted or built locally, could address them? Drawing directly on operational experience at Kumbatia Seafood.
2. Representation gaps in global AI governance frameworks
Major AI governance frameworks (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, OECD AI Principles) were developed with limited Global South input. This project examines where they fail to apply to lower-income country contexts and what policy mechanisms could improve inclusion.
3. AI risk assessment in critical infrastructure, developing economy contexts
Developing practical frameworks for assessing AI-related risks in critical infrastructure sectors where institutional oversight capacity is lower, with East Africa as the primary case study.
